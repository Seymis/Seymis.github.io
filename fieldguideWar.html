“Royal Park was the site of a major Australian military camp and training ground
during the First World War (1914-1918) and was used for the mobilisation of ANZACs
to the Western Front. It was also a camp for both Australian and US troops during
the Second World War prior to their deployment to Pacific War campaigns (1941-1945).”
-- (Royal Park 2018)
